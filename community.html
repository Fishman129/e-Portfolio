Community Engagement

At Texas A&M International University I am part of the Pre Law Society where I have the opportunity to connect with others with a similar major and aspirations, and learn from weekly meetings with special invitees and events.

Reflection

Being part of this society has helped me come out of my shell in so many aspects. I have the opportunity to attend alongside my brother, and together we learn and connect with others every week. From attorneys giving presentations to trips to local courthouses, being part of the Pre Law Society helped me grow as a Political Science major. Hearing from their experiences, seeing the real world in action, and seeing other students just like me, it has all allowed me to expand my points of view. Overall, it has reinforced my desire to go to law school, become an attorney, but most importantly, keep making connections and expand my network. Thankfully it has opened a lot of doors for me -such as internships-, and will continue to do so as I keep engaging with others. Reflecting on this helped me see that community engagement is not only a good skill to have, but a necessity in today's world. I am glad I have experienced this early on in my academic life, and I will continue looking for opportunities just like joining this society was for me. I am aware of the value being a social species holds, and I am determined to use every opportunity thrown at me to the best of my ability.
